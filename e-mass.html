Healthcare Technology

E-MASS

The Electronic Medical Appointment Scheduling System is a robust digital platform designed to optimize patient flow and reduce wait times in medical facilities.

Modernizing Healthcare Access

Manual appointment booking often leads to overbooked slots and patient frustration. E-MASS provides a digital solution that coordinates patient needs with provider availability in real-time.

Smart Scheduling

Automated slot management that prevents double-booking and allows patients to choose available times from their own devices.

Patient Records

Secure digital profiles that store appointment history, making it easier for clinics to track patient visits over time.

Queue Management

Real-time updates on appointment status to help medical staff manage daily patient loads efficiently.

Technical Infrastructure

E-MASS utilizes a secure database architecture to ensure that sensitive medical appointment data remains private while being easily accessible to authorized medical personnel.

  • Cloud-Based Sync: Ensures schedules are updated across all devices instantly.
  • Automated Reminders: Designed to support future integration for SMS or email notifications to reduce "no-shows."
  • Responsive Interface: Optimized for use by both clinic administrators and patients on mobile or desktop.

Specifications

  • PHP Backend
  • MySQL Database
  • Secure Patient Data Auth
  • Mobile-First UI
