BIRTS

The Barangay Inhabitant Registration and Transaction System (BIRTS) is a comprehensive management platform designed to digitize and streamline the operations of a local barangay. Built on a centralized digital database to eliminate manual redundancy.

Resident Profiles
Barangay Documents
Case Management

Digitalizing the Barangay

Traditional barangay record-keeping often suffers from data redundancy and slow manual processing. BIRTS provides a unified platform to manage inhabitant data, official registries, and daily transactions with high precision.

Resident Profile Management

Centralized database for registering profiles, demographic data, and contact details for secure verification.

Barangay Clearance

Quick issuance of clearances with automated record-tracking and a full digital history for every resident.

Business Management

Processes and monitors Business Clearances, tracking owner info and compliance for local establishments.

Indigent Identification

Identify residents qualified for social assistance, relief distribution, and welfare monitoring effectively.

Family Registry

Groups residents by household units to track family structures, essential for disaster response planning.

Blotter Case Recording

Official digital logbook for recording incidents and disputes with detailed status tracking for the Lupon.

Digital ID Generation

Create printable or digital Barangay IDs with photos for secure inhabitant identification.

Reporting & Analytics

Generates statistical data on population and clearances to help officials make data-driven decisions.

System Architecture

BIRTS is built using a modular PHP architecture, ensuring that each component can be updated without disrupting services.

  • Unified Database: Central repository for all records.
  • Role-Based Access: Secure logins for staff and admins.
  • Verification System: Prevents data duplication.
  • Audit Logs: Full accountability for modifications.

Specifications

  • PHP / Laravel
  • MySQL Relational DB
  • Local & Cloud Ready
  • Data Encryption
