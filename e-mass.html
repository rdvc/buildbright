E-MASS

The Electronic Medical Appointment Scheduling System is a robust digital platform designed to optimize patient flow, reduce wait times, and provide seamless doctor-patient coordination.

Modernizing Healthcare Access

Manual appointment booking often leads to overbooked slots and patient frustration. E-MASS provides a digital solution that coordinates patient needs with provider availability in real-time.

Patient Dashboard

Personalized overview for patients to manage bookings, track upcoming schedules, and search for providers in one view.

Doctor & Session Search

Advanced filtering to find medical professionals by specialty, profile details, or specific consultation time slots.

Online Booking System

Patients can select and confirm appointment slots digitally, eliminating the need for physical visits just to secure a schedule.

Booking Management

A "My Bookings" module allowing patients to review session types, assigned doctors, and full appointment histories.

Doctor Directory

Complete access to professional profiles, specialties, and real-time availability for all registered medical staff.

Real-Time Monitoring

Live tracking of total registered patients, new bookings, and scheduled sessions for efficient facility administration.

Secure Authentication

Encrypted login protocols ensuring that sensitive medical schedules and personal data remain strictly confidential.

Profile Management

Self-service module for patients to update personal information, medical preferences, and account settings.

Appointment Tracking

Visual indicators and countdowns for upcoming consultations to reduce missed appointments and optimize clinic flow.

Technical Infrastructure

E-MASS utilizes a secure database architecture to ensure that sensitive medical appointment data remains private while being easily accessible to authorized personnel.

  • Cloud-Based Sync: Real-time schedule updates across all devices.
  • Conflict Resolution: Logic-driven backend to prevent double-booking of doctors or sessions.
  • Responsive Interface: Optimized for use by both clinic administrators and mobile patients.
  • Session Tracking: Detailed logging of consultation statuses from "Pending" to "Completed."

Specifications

  • PHP Backend
  • MySQL Relational DB
  • SSL/AES Data Security
  • Mobile-First UI
