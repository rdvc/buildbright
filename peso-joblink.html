System Case Study

PESO JobLink

An integrated LGU employment ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between local talent and industry demand through automated intelligence.

Administrative Command Center

LGU Governance & Oversight

Matching Engine

Sophisticated scoring algorithm that ranks job seekers based on skills overlap, educational attainment, and geographic location (Barangay-level matching).

Accreditation System

Document verification pipeline for employers. Admin can review business permits and logos to ensure only legitimate companies post vacancies.

Labor Analytics

Real-time reporting on hiring rates, labor supply (seekers) vs demand (vacancies), and gender-disaggregated employment data.

User Security

Global user management with the ability to Activate, Deactivate, or Suspend accounts to maintain a safe ecosystem.

Application Tracking

Monitor every applicant's journey from "Pending" to "Hired" across all city employers in one centralized view.

Employer Portal

Industry Recruitment Tools

Vacancy Management

Post detailed job openings with specific skill requirements, slot counts, and educational criteria.

Applicant Screening

View full seeker profiles, educational history, and work experience before making hiring decisions.

One-Click Hiring

Update application statuses instantly to "Interview Scheduled", "Hired", or "Rejected", triggering real-time notifications.

Job Seeker Experience

Candidate Growth & Networking

Digital Resume

Integrated profile showcasing education, technical skills, and seminars/trainings.

Seeker Network

Social features to connect and network with other job seekers within the same city.

Real-time Chat

Internal messaging system with image attachment support for peer-to-peer communication.

Job Notifications

Instant alerts when an employer updates an application status or shortlists a candidate.

Infrastructure Stack

PHP 8.x
MySQL
Vanilla JS
Tailwind CSS
