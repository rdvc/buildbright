Digitizing Community Living

Managing a subdivision manually leads to billing disputes and disorganized records. Community Hub automates core operations, ensuring financial transparency and efficient resident service delivery through a centralized dashboard.

Point of Sale (POS)

Integrated collection system for real-time processing of association dues, penalties, and service fees with automated receipting.

Geo-Mapping & Tagging

Interactive village map for visual property tracking, allowing admins to tag units based on occupancy and ownership status.

Household Registry

Advanced resident list management that groups individuals by household units for more accurate community demographics.

Unit Inventory

Track real-time availability of residential units, categorizing them as Available, Occupied, or Sold directly on the dashboard.

Billing & Payment History

Comprehensive ledger for every property, maintaining a secure record of all historical transactions and outstanding balances.

Maintenance Module

Dedicated tools for managing community facility repairs, equipment inventory, and work order scheduling.

Technical Infrastructure

Community Hub focuses on data integrity. By utilizing a secure relational database, the system ensures that every "Today's Collection" entry is logged and auditable.

  • Administrative Quick Actions: High-speed navigation for core tasks like owner registration and billing.
  • Dynamic Status Indicators: Real-time dashboard cards for resident counts and unit availability.
  • Financial Logic: Multi-layer math engine for calculating monthly dues and penalties.
  • Geospatial Integration: Plots property coordinates onto a custom village layout for easier management.
