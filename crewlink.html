Streamlining Global Maritime Operations

Managing large-scale maritime crews involves complex rotations and strict regulatory compliance. CrewLink digitizes the entire lifecycle of crew management, ensuring data-driven decision making for fleet managers.

Dashboard Overview

Real-time "Fleet Intelligence" reports providing a snapshot of active sessions, sailing crew, and deployment readiness.

Visa & Compliance Alerts

Automated tracking of crew documentation with high-priority alerts for visas expiring within 6 months.

Vessel Management

Comprehensive tracking of fleet status, categorizing vessels by active deployment, maintenance, or inactive units.

Personnel Management

A centralized crew list to manage profiles, embarkation history, and "Onsigner/Offsigner" rotation status.

PVC ID System

Integrated module to generate professional maritime ID cards with dynamic QR codes for secure port verification.

Access Monitoring

Audit logs tracking "Recent System Access" to maintain high-level security and administrative accountability.

Technical Infrastructure

CrewLink is built on a scalable PHP architecture designed to handle global synchronization between shore-based offices and active vessels.

  • Dynamic QR Verification: Secure ID authentication for personnel.
  • Automated Rotation Logic: Tracks sailing vs. offsigner status to prevent staffing gaps.
  • Real-time Analytics: Instant processing of fleet-wide intelligence reports.
  • Compliance Engine: Proactive alerting for expiring maritime certifications and visas.

Specifications

  • PHP Custom Framework
  • MySQL Relational DB
  • Dynamic QR Engine
  • Global Data Sync
