TENANT BILL

The Tenant Billing System (TBS) is a powerful utility and rent management platform. Designed for property owners, mall operators, and apartment managers to automate monthly collections, monitor arrears, and manage property occupancy with zero stress.

Property Management Simplified

Chasing payments and manually computing electric bills is a thing of the past. Tenant Bill provides a systematic way to track every centavo, ensuring your cash flow remains healthy and your records transparent.

Tenant Profiling

Maintain detailed records of tenants, including contract terms, security deposits, and valid identification.

Utility Metering

Input current readings for electricity and water. The system automatically calculates consumption based on set rates.

Automated Due Dates

Set recurring monthly rent. The system automatically generates new bills on your preferred cut-off dates.

Arrears Tracking

Instantly see who is overdue. The system tracks unpaid balances and automatically carries them over to the next month.

E-Invoices

Generate clean, printable PDF invoices or send billing summaries via SMS and email to tenants.

Payment Collection

Record partial or full payments. Support for cash, bank transfers, and e-wallets with digital acknowledgment receipts.

Occupancy Monitoring

Visual dashboard showing which units are occupied, vacant, or under maintenance for better property planning.

Collection Analytics

View monthly income reports, collection efficiency, and projected revenue for the coming months.

System Architecture

The Tenant Billing System is built for long-term data retention and high accuracy. It utilizes a transactional ledger system where every financial entry is double-checked against the tenant's balance.

  • Dynamic Rates: Easily update KWH or Cubic Meter rates as utility prices change.
  • Contract Management: Track start and end dates with alerts for expiring leases.
  • Multi-Property Support: Manage different buildings or locations from a single admin account.

Specifications

  • PHP Laravel
  • MySQL / MariaDB
  • PDF Engine for Bills
  • Encrypted Payment Logs
