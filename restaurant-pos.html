REST POS

The Restaurant Point of Sale Solution (REST POS) is a specialized management ecosystem designed to synchronize front-of-house service with kitchen operations and inventory. Built for high-volume efficiency and zero-error order routing.

Floor Management
Kitchen Routing
Live Inventory

Modernizing Food Service

Manual ordering and paper slips slow down your service. REST POS digitizes the entire lifecycle of a customer order—from the first drink at the table to the final stock deduction in the pantry.

Visual Floor Map

Dynamic table mapping with real-time status indicators (Available, Occupied, Dirty, Reserved).

Tableside Ordering

Direct entry via tablets instantly routes orders to kitchen printers or display screens.

Smart Billing

Effortlessly split bills by item, seat, or percentage. Supports G-Cash, Card, and Cash payments.

Kitchen Display (KDS)

Digital tickets for chefs with timers and priority alerts to reduce ticket times.

Ingredient Tracking

Recipe-based inventory deductions. Track every gram of stock used in real-time.

Tax & Discounts

Automated Senior Citizen, PWD, and promotional discount calculations for legal compliance.

Z-Reading Reports

Generate daily sales summaries, VAT reports, and staff performance analytics with one click.

Offline Sync

Keep taking orders even if the internet fails. Data automatically syncs once reconnected.

System Architecture

REST POS utilizes a high-performance PHP/Laravel architecture designed for concurrent user requests. Every transaction is logged with millisecond precision to ensure data integrity.

  • Distributed Printing: Print to specific kitchen/bar stations simultaneously.
  • Local Network Support: Low-latency operations within the restaurant LAN.
  • Secure Audit Trail: Track every voided item or modified transaction.

Specifications

  • Laravel Framework
  • MySQL Relational DB
  • ESC/POS Printer Support
  • Role-Based Access
