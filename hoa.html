Technical Infrastructure

Community Hub focuses on data integrity and financial accuracy. The system ensures that every collection entry is logged, timestamped, and fully auditable by association officers.

  • Financial Logic: Multi-layer engine for calculating dues, arrears, and penalties.
  • GIS Integration: Plots property coordinates onto custom village layouts for admins.
  • Audit Trail: Full transparency for every record modification or payment entry.
