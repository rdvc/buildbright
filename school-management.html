The School Management System (SMS) is an all-in-one administrative platform designed to modernize educational workflows. From paperless enrollment to automated grading and student portals, we simplify the complex operations of modern schools.

Streamlining Education

Manual record-keeping in schools is prone to loss and error. Bright School centralizes student data, ensuring that teachers, administrators, and parents have real-time access to critical academic information.

Digital Enrollment

Automated admission process with document uploading and student profile creation to reduce long lines.

Attendance Tracking

Track daily student attendance with automated SMS notifications for parents when a student is absent.

Automated Grading

Easy encoding for teachers with automated calculation of GWA and ranking. Generates printable report cards.

Financial Dashboard

Manage tuition fees, miscellaneous charges, and scholarship grants with full payment history and receipts.

Faculty Portal

Dedicated space for teachers to manage schedules, lesson plans, and student performance metrics.

Library Management

Digital cataloging of books with borrower tracking, return dates, and fine calculations.

Student Portal

Secure login for students to view grades, download materials, and check their current financial balance.

Certificate Generation

One-click generation of Certificates of Enrollment, Good Moral, and Transcript of Records (TOR).

System Architecture

Bright School utilizes a secure multi-tenant architecture, ensuring that student data remains private and protected while being accessible across local networks or cloud servers.

  • Encrypted Data: High-level protection for sensitive personal information.
  • Automated Backups: Daily system snapshots to prevent data loss.
  • Responsive UI: Accessible via school desktops, tablets, or mobile phones.

Specifications

  • Laravel Framework
  • MySQL Relational DB
  • API-Ready Architecture
  • SSL / Role-Based Access
