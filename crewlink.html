Logistics & Personnel Management

Crewlink Management System

A comprehensive ERP solution designed to manage maritime and industrial crew links, optimizing personnel tracking, contract automation, and deployment scheduling.

Streamlining Global Logistics

Managing large-scale crews often involves complex scheduling and heavy paperwork. CMS digitizes the entire lifecycle of crew management, ensuring that personnel are deployed efficiently and in compliance with maritime regulations.

Personnel Tracking

Real-time monitoring of crew status, certifications, and deployment history to ensure readiness.

Contract Automation

Automated generation and tracking of employment contracts, reducing manual entry errors.

Deployment Scheduling

Advanced scheduling tools that manage rotation cycles and prevent staffing gaps on active vessels.

Technical Infrastructure

CMS is built to handle high volumes of data across global networks. It utilizes a secure relational database to manage complex relationships between personnel, vessels, and contracts.

  • Encrypted Data Storage: Protects sensitive personnel and financial information.
  • Automated Notifications: Alerts administrators to expiring certifications or contract renewals.
  • Scalable Architecture: Designed to grow with the company's fleet size and personnel count.

Specifications

  • PHP Backend
  • MySQL Relational DB
  • PDF Automation Engine
  • Global Cloud Integration
